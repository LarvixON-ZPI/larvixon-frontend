## 2. What Data We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information (Personal Data)
When you register, we collect:
- **Email address** (used for login and communication)
- **Password** (hashed using Argon2, never stored in plain text)
- **First name and last name**
- **Organization** (optional, e.g., hospital, research institute)
- **Bio/Description** (optional)

**Legal basis:** Contractual necessity (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b))

### 2.2 Medical Data (Special Category Data under GDPR)
When you upload an analysis, we collect:
- **Video files** of larvae exposed to patient plasma samples
- **Analysis metadata:** upload date, analysis name, status
- **AI-generated results:** detected substances, confidence scores

⚠️ **Important:** The videos may indirectly contain health-related data about patients (through plasma exposure). We do NOT collect direct patient identifiers (name, ID, medical record number).

**Legal basis:** 
- Explicit consent (GDPR Art. 9(2)(a))
- Scientific research purposes (GDPR Art. 9(2)(j)) if applicable

### 2.3 Technical Data (Automatically Collected)
- **IP address** (for security, rate limiting)
- **Browser type and version**
- **Device information** (OS, screen size)
- **Cookies and session tokens** (JWT for authentication)
- **API request logs** (timestamp, endpoint, status code)

**Legal basis:** Legitimate interest (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f))

## 6. Your Rights Under GDPR

### 6.1 Right to Access (Art. 15)
Request a copy of all data we hold about you.

### 6.2 Right to Rectification (Art. 16)
Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.

### 6.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten") (Art. 17)
Request deletion of your account and all associated data.

### 6.4 Right to Data Portability (Art. 20)
Receive your data in machine-readable format (JSON/CSV).

### 6.5 Right to Object (Art. 21)
Object to processing based on legitimate interest.

### 6.6 Right to Lodge a Complaint
File a complaint with your national Data Protection Authority.  
**Poland:** UODO (Urząd Ochrony Danych Osobowych) - uodo.gov.pl

**How to exercise rights:** Contact larvixonhelp@gmail.com
